iYang 爱养生 - 隐私政策

最后更新：2026年4月9日

1. 概述

iYang（爱养生）非常重视您的隐私保护。本隐私政策说明了我们如何处理您的数据。

2. 数据收集

  • 不收集任何个人健康数据：我们不会收集、存储或上传您的任何个人健康数据到云端服务器
  • 完全本地存储：所有照片、健康记录、语音数据、问卷回答仅存储在您的设备本地
  • 不上传云端：所有分析、处理都在您的设备本地完成，数据不离开您的手机

3. 数据使用

  • 照片仅用于本地分析：舌象、面色照片仅在您的设备上进行分析
  • 健康数据仅用于本地显示和建议：HealthKit 数据仅用于在您的设备上显示和生成养生建议
  • 不与任何第三方共享数据：我们不会与任何第三方共享您的任何数据

4. 数据安全

  • 所有数据存储在设备本地：使用 iOS 系统的沙箱机制保护数据安全
  • 使用系统加密保护数据：利用设备的加密功能保护您的数据
  • 用户可随时删除所有数据：您可以在应用中随时查看、导出或删除所有个人数据

5. 用户权利

  • 查看数据：您可以随时在应用中查看所有个人数据
  • 导出数据：您可以随时导出所有个人数据
  • 删除数据：您可以随时删除所有个人数据

6. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何问题，请通过以下方式联系我们：

  • 应用内反馈：在应用的"我的"页面中使用反馈功能

iYang Wellness - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 9, 2026

1. Overview

iYang Wellness takes your privacy very seriously. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data Collection

  • No personal health data collected: We do not collect, store, or upload any of your personal health data to cloud servers
  • Complete local storage: All photos, health records, voice data, and questionnaire responses are stored locally on your device only
  • No cloud upload: All analysis and processing are done locally on your device, data never leaves your phone

3. Data Usage

  • Photos for local analysis only: Tongue and face photos are analyzed only on your device
  • Health data for local display and suggestions only: HealthKit data is used only for display and wellness suggestions on your device
  • No data sharing with third parties: We do not share any of your data with any third parties

4. Data Security

  • All data stored locally on device: Uses iOS sandbox mechanism to protect data security
  • System encryption to protect data: Leverages device encryption to protect your data
  • Users can delete all data at any time: You can view, export, or delete all personal data in the app at any time

5. User Rights

  • View data: You can view all personal data in the app at any time
  • Export data: You can export all personal data at any time
  • Delete data: You can delete all personal data at any time

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through:

  • In-app feedback: Use the feedback function in the "Me" page of the app
