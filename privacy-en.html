iYang Wellness - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 9, 2026

1. Overview

iYang (爱养生) takes your privacy very seriously. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data Collection

  • No personal health data collected: We do not collect, store, or upload any of your personal health data to cloud servers
  • Completely local storage: All photos, health records, voice data, and questionnaire responses are stored only locally on your device
  • No cloud upload: All analysis and processing is done locally on your device; data never leaves your phone

3. Data Usage

  • Photos for local analysis only: Tongue and face photos are analyzed only on your device
  • Voice data not uploaded: Voice data is processed locally and not uploaded
  • Questionnaire data stored locally: All questionnaire responses are stored only on your device
  • No third-party sharing: We do not share any data with third parties

4. Data Security

  • Local storage protection: All data is stored in the app's sandbox directory
  • System encryption: Leverages device encryption to protect your data
  • Users can delete all data: You can view, export, or delete all personal data in the app at any time

5. User Rights

  • View data: You can view all personal data in the app at any time
  • Export data: You can export all personal data at any time
  • Delete data: You can delete all personal data at any time

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@iyang.app
