Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

1. Overview

iYang takes your privacy very seriously. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

🌿 Core Principle: All personal health data is stored only on your device and never uploaded to any server

2. Data Collection

  • HealthKit Data: The app reads your steps, heart rate, sleep and other HealthKit data for display only, never uploaded to any server
  • Completely local storage: All health records are stored only locally on your device
  • No cloud upload: All data is processed locally on your device and never leaves your phone

3. Data Usage

  • HealthKit data for display only: Data read from HealthKit is only displayed within the app, no analysis or diagnosis is performed
  • No third-party sharing: We do not share any data with third parties
  • No AI analysis: The app does not provide AI analysis features; all data is for informational display only

4. Data Security

  • Local storage protection: All data is stored in the app's sandbox directory
  • System encryption: Leverages device encryption to protect your data
  • Users can delete all data: You can view, export, or delete all personal data in the app at any time

5. User Rights

  • View data: You can view all personal data in the app at any time
  • Export data: You can export all personal data at any time
  • Delete data: You can delete all personal data at any time

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@iyang.app
